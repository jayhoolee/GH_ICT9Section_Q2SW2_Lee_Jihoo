Dog Breeds

Image Breed description
Pomeranian Pomeranians are small dogs but do not act that way. They may challenge larger dogs. They make a good apartment pet and may also bark a lot. All this dog needs is love and affection.
Golden Retriever Golden Retrievers are very friendly, playful and have an extroverted personality. They have high intelligence and energy.
Shiba Inu An ancient Japanese breed, the Shiba Inu is a little but well-muscled dog once exmployed as a hunter. Today it is the most popular companion dog in Japan.
